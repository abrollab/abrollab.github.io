# Hero Slideshow Images

Place your slideshow images here. The hero section will automatically display them in a rotating slideshow.

## Naming Convention
- slide1.jpg
- slide2.jpg
- slide3.jpg
- etc.

## Recommended Specifications
- **Dimensions**: 1920x1080px (Full HD)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9
- **Format**: JPG
- **File Size**: Under 500KB each (after compression)
- **Quality**: 80-90% JPG quality

## Image Ideas
- Laboratory equipment and workspace
- Team members collaborating
- Research in action
- Scientific instruments and experiments
- Campus or building exterior
- Close-ups of research materials

## Free Stock Photo Resources
If you need placeholder images:
- Unsplash: https://unsplash.com/s/photos/laboratory
- Pexels: https://www.pexels.com/search/science/
- Pixabay: https://pixabay.com/images/search/research/

## Current Setup
The slideshow will automatically detect and display all JPG/PNG images in this directory. Add at least 3-5 images for the best effect.
